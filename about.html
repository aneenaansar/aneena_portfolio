Who I Am

Hello, I'm Aneena, a dedicated Full-Stack Web Developer and Digital Marketing Specialist who blends technical expertise with creative strategy to craft impactful digital solutions. I believe that every line of code and every marketing campaign tells a story – and my goal is to make that story engaging, functional, and results-driven.

My web development expertise covers HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Django, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, and ASP.NET. I create responsive, high-performance websites and web apps, integrate secure backend systems, and design user-friendly interfaces. I also work extensively with databases including SQL and PostgreSQL, and manage projects using GitHub for version control.

On the marketing side, I specialize in building strong online presences through SEO, Content Marketing, Social Media Campaigns, Email Marketing, Google Ads, and PPC strategies. My toolkit includes industry-standard platforms such as Google Analytics, SEMRush, Ahrefs, HubSpot, Mailchimp, and Hootsuite to ensure each campaign is measurable and effective.

I’m passionate about bridging the gap between development and marketing – combining clean, optimized code with powerful digital strategies to build experiences that engage users and drive results. Whether it’s developing a custom web application or crafting a data-driven marketing plan, I approach every project with creativity, precision, and a focus on delivering value.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, experimenting with creative design ideas, and continuously learning to stay ahead in this fast-paced digital world.
